For My Love ❤️

I’ve always known that life might take us in different directions, and I respect the choices you need to make, even if it hurts me deeply. The truth is, I’ll never stop loving you no matter what happens. You are not just the love of my life, but also the best person I’ve ever known. My greatest friend, my safe place, my heart.

When the time comes for you to walk a different path, I’ll be broken, and I might either drift away or lock myself away. But you have to keep me with you. You’ll have to rebuild me, piece by piece, and keep me together because without you, I don’t know if I’ll ever feel whole again.

Even if I can’t have you the way I wish, I’ll always be here for you, because in this messed up world I’ve been lucky enough to find not just love, but the best friend I could ever ask for. And no matter what, that will never change.

I love you, sweetheart. ❤️
